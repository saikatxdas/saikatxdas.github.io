Research

I study processes in high-energy and multi-messenger astrophysics. In my PhD, I studied ultrahigh-energy cosmic rays, their origin and propagation, gamma-ray emission from blazars and their correlation with high-energy neutrino observations. Currently I am working on probing the multi-messenger signals from heavy dark matter decay.
